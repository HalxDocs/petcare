Our Passion for Pet Care

Dedicated to creating the safest, most effective hygiene products for animals since 2010

From Clinic to Shelves

Founded by Dr. Samantha Green, a veterinary dermatologist with over 20 years of experience, PetGuard Pro began as a solution to the harsh chemical products she saw causing skin irritation in her patients.

What started as small-batch formulas for her clinic patients has grown into a trusted brand used by veterinarians, groomers, and pet owners worldwide.
