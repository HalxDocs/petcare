Delivery FAQs

What areas do you deliver to?

We offer nationwide delivery to all 50 states, including Alaska and Hawaii. We also ship to Puerto Rico and most US territories.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery (available for an additional fee) arrives in 1-2 business days. Delivery times may vary for remote areas.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat $5.99 shipping fee applies.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email that lets you follow your package every step of the way.
