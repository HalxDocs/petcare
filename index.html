Our Story

Founded in 2010 by veterinary dermatologist Dr. Samantha Green, PetGuard Pro began with a simple mission: to create safe, effective hygiene products that truly care for animals' skin and coat health.

After years of seeing pets suffer from harsh chemical products, Dr. Green assembled a team of scientists and animal care experts to develop formulas that clean and protect without irritation.

Today, PetGuard Pro is trusted by veterinarians, groomers, and pet owners worldwide, with products for every animal need.
